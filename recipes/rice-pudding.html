Rice Pudding

Description

A recipe to make old-fashioned rice pudding, with cooked rice, egg, butter and vanilla. Yum yum.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups cooked rice
  • 2 cups milk, divided
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • ⅓ cup white sugar
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine cooked rice, 1 1/2 cups milk, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir until thick and creamy, 15 to 20 minutes.
  2. Stir remaining 1/2 cup milk, golden raisins, beaten egg, and white sugar into the rice mixture; stirring continually. Continue cooking until egg is set, 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Remove saucepan from heat; stir butter and vanilla extract into the pudding.
